3、Add cool new visualizations to Totem/Rhythmbox(为Totem/Rhythmbox添加非常酷的视觉效果)
Use Synaptic to search for and install the libvisual-0.4-plugins package.
（用新立得搜索并安装libvisual-0.4-plugins。）
In Totem,click Edit ! Preferences,select the Display tab in the dialog that appears, and make your choicefrom the Type of visualization dropdown list.
（Totem,依次点击“编辑-首选项-显示”，在“可视化类型”的下拉菜单里选择你喜欢的。）
In RhythmBox, click View ! Visualization to start the animation and then select from the dropdown list beneath the visualization
（RhythmBox,点击“察看"勾选“可视化”。）
